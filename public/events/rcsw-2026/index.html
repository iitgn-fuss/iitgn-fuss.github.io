Research on Computer Systems Workshop

21st February 2026 (Saturday)
An outreach event hosted by the Department of CSE at IIT Gandhinagar
Join us for Research on Computer Systems Workshop (RCSW) 2026, an one-day hybrid event showcasing research opportunities in computer systems through talks, networking opportunities, and a panel discussion.
Target Audience

The workshop is designed for students, researchers, and industry professionals interested in exploring research on computer systems. Whether you're considering graduate studies, looking for collaborations, or simply curious about current research trends, RCSW 2026 offers insights into the diverse landscape of computer systems research.

About

The workshop is co-organized by Nipun Batra, Abhishek Bichhawat, Neeldhara Misra, on behalf of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Gandhinagar. We are grateful to be supported by a Google Explore CSR grant and IIT Gandhinagar.

For any inquiries, please contact us at cse@iitgn.ac.in with RCSW 2026 Inquiry as the subject.
