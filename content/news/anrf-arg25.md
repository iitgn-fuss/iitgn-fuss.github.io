We are delighted to share that Prof. Abhishek Bichhawat, in collaboration with [Prof. Mainack Mondal from IIT Kharagpur](https://cse.iitkgp.ac.in/~mainack/), has been awarded the [ANRF ARG Research Grant](https://anrfonline.in/ANRF/arg_anrf) for their proposal on designing a usable and privacy-compliant consent management framework for online browsing. 

Read more about the award on Prof. Mainack Mondal's LinkedIn post: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mainack-mondal-73a1427_anrf-arg-anrf-activity-7408063928557023232-4jVN
